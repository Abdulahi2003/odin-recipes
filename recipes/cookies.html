Chocolate chip cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are iconic treats cherished for their comforting aroma and delightful flavor. They are made from a dough infused with chocolate chips, yeilding a perfect balance of sweet and salty notes. Bakes to golden perfection, each cookie boasts a crisp edge and a chewy center, creating a satisfying texture that melts in the mouth. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven with a glass of milk or packed as a portable snack, chocolate chip cookies evoke nostalgia and joy with every delicious bit.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chip.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Baking until the edges are golden brown.
