Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a savory Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella. Baked to perfection, each bite offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, from the tender pasta sheets to the hearty meat filling and cheese topping. It's a comforting and satisfying meal that has become a timeless favorite around the world.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses(cottage cheese, mozarella, and parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, s cups of mozarella, eggs, and half of the parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
